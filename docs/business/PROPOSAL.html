1 Executive Summary

P.W. Nigeria Limited manages over 2,000 pieces of heavy equipment and plant machinery across 30+ construction sites nationwide. Currently, tracking plant locations, maintenance expenditure, weekly operational reports, and spare parts procurement relies on manual processes — Excel spreadsheets, phone calls, and paper-based reporting.

I have developed a purpose-built digital management system that gives P.W. Nigeria management instant, real-time visibility into every piece of equipment the company owns — where it is, what condition it’s in, how much it costs to maintain, and who is supplying parts.

This proposal outlines the system’s capabilities, the business value it delivers, and a recommended engagement structure.

2.2 The cost of not knowing

For a company managing 2,000+ heavy equipment assets worth billions of naira:

  • Equipment downtime due to delayed maintenance decisions costs an estimated 2–5% of fleet value annually
  • Spare parts overspend from duplicate purchases and no supplier comparison — estimated 10–20% waste
  • Lost or misallocated equipment between 30+ sites with no digital trail
  • Management decisions delayed because data takes days or weeks to compile
  • Weekly reports from 30+ sites arrive in different formats, contain errors, require manual reconciliation
Conservative estimate: Even a 3% improvement in equipment utilization and maintenance efficiency across 2,000+ plants translates to tens of millions of naira in annual savings.

I have built a complete, production-ready digital management system specifically designed around P.W. Nigeria’s actual workflows, report formats, and operational needs.

3.2 Key Capabilities

Instant Answers:

  • Search any plant by fleet number → see current location, condition, full movement history, maintenance costs
  • Filter by location, fleet type, condition, date range — in seconds
  • Click on any site → see every plant there, their status, and costs

Automated Data Processing:

  • Upload weekly Excel reports → system automatically extracts and processes plant data
  • Validation rules catch errors before data enters the system
  • No more manual data entry from 30+ weekly spreadsheets

Cost Intelligence:

  • Total spare parts spend by month, quarter, year
  • Compare current year vs. previous year — are costs trending up or down?
  • Top 10 highest-cost plants and top 10 suppliers by spend
  • Track every purchase order with itemized costs

Management Reports (One Click):

  • Fleet summary by location and condition
  • Plant verification and compliance rates
  • Maintenance cost analysis with flexible grouping
  • Export data for further analysis
